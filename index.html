About

We are an AI-powered digital media company creating original children's and family content for global audiences. Led by an exceptional team of industry experts and storytellers, combining creative excellence and years of industry experience to build scalable, multi-platform brands. Our IP are designed to entertain, inspire and deliver lasting impact. Blending imagination with purpose to help children learn, grow and explore, from concept to screen and beyond.
